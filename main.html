Maldives


The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean, famous for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, luxurious resorts, and vibrant marine life. It is an ideal destination for relaxation, adventure, and experiencing nature’s beauty at its finest.

  1. Male City — Explore the capital city’s vibrant markets and historic mosques.
  2. Maafushi Island – Enjoy adventurous water sports and local island culture.
  3. Banana Reef – Discover the underwater beauty with world-class diving and snorkeling.
  4. Hulhumale Beach – Relax on the serene shores and watch the sunset.
  5. Vaadhoo Island – Witness the magical bioluminescent sea, glowing under the night sky.
  • Mas Huni – A traditional breakfast dish made with tuna, coconut, and onions.
  • Garudhiya – A flavorful fish soup served with rice and lime.
